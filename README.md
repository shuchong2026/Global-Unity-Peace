1. 战争是人类文明尚未成熟的体现——如同小孩子打架，成熟的文明应通过沟通解决分歧。

4. 停止内部消耗、团结一致对外探索星海——是人类文明唯一的救赎之道。

地球是原始资金，全人类是原始股东

现在，我想用做生意的方式来给大家算一笔账。

假如把地球看作是一笔原始资金，全人类就是这笔资金的原始股东。

这笔资金是多少？就是地球上的所有资源——土地、矿产、石油、天然气、森林、水源。这就是我们全部的原始资本。

可现在的问题是：股东们不好好想着怎么用这笔钱去投资、去创业、去赚更多的钱，反而在公司内部打起来了。你抢我的股份，我夺你的资产，你占了我的地，我就要炸了你的矿。

打来打去，公司的资产缩水了，股价跌了，所有人都亏了。本来可以用来投资未来的钱，全浪费在内耗上了。

真正聪明的股东会怎么做？

第一，守住自己现有的股份。 你现在有多少，就是多少。谁也不许抢谁的。

第二，停止内耗。 把打架的钱省下来，用作公司的研发经费。

第三，也是最重要的——用这笔原始资金，去投资一个前所未有的项目。

这个项目叫什么？叫星际探索。

这个项目的回报是什么？是整个宇宙。

银河系有一千亿颗恒星。每一颗恒星周围，都可能有多颗行星。那些行星上有取之不尽的资源，有用之不竭的能量，有广阔无垠的生存空间。

只要我们停止内耗，把所有的资金、技术、人才都投入到这个项目上——

终有一天，公司会上市。而这个公司的市值，将超出所有人的想象。

到那个时候，每一个原始股东——也就是地球上的每一个人——都将获得一笔巨大的分红。

这笔分红是什么？

是一颗属于自己的星球。

你喜欢山，就分一颗多山的星球。你喜欢海，就分一颗全是水的星球。你喜欢安静，就分一颗远离喧嚣的星球。

我预言，终会有那么一天，就会实现一人一个星球的理想愿望。

到那个时候，再回过头来看今天的地球——那些为了几块油田、几条边境线打了几十年的仗，会不会觉得特别可笑？

所以，不要再打了。地球是我们的原始资金，不是我们的战场。守住它，用它去投资星辰大海。

那里，才是人类真正的未来。

Earth as Startup Capital, Humanity as Original Shareholders

Now, let me put it in business terms.

Imagine Earth as startup capital. All of humanity are the original shareholders.

How much capital do we have? All of Earth's resources — land, minerals, oil, natural gas, forests, water. That is our total initial investment.

But here's the problem: instead of figuring out how to invest this capital, grow the business, and make more money, the shareholders are fighting among themselves. You grab my shares. I seize your assets. You occupy my land. I blow up your mines.

Back and forth, the company's assets shrink. The stock price crashes. Everyone loses. Money that could have been used to invest in the future is wasted on internal conflict.

What would smart shareholders do?

First, hold on to your current shares. Whatever you have now is yours. No one takes from anyone else.

Second, stop the infighting. Take the money you were spending on fighting and put it into R&D.

Third — and most importantly — use this startup capital to invest in a project unlike any before.

What project? Interstellar exploration.

What's the return on this investment? The entire universe.

The Milky Way has a hundred billion stars. Around each star, there may be multiple planets. Those planets contain inexhaustible resources, limitless energy, and vast expanses of living space.

If we stop fighting among ourselves and pour all our capital, technology, and talent into this project —

One day, the company will go public. And its market value will exceed anything we can imagine.

At that moment, every original shareholder — every person on Earth — will receive a tremendous dividend.

What is that dividend?

A planet of your own.

If you love mountains, you get a planet full of mountains. If you love the ocean, you get a water world. If you love solitude, you get a quiet planet far from the noise.

I prophesy that one day, the dream of one person, one planet will come true.

When that day comes, look back at Earth today — at the wars fought over oil fields and borderlines for decades. Won't it all seem ridiculous?

So stop fighting. Earth is our startup capital, not our battlefield. Protect it. Use it to invest in the sea of stars.

That is where humanity's true future lies.
